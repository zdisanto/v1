An examination of the physical and biological mechanisms that underlie or
are associated with specific behaviors and experiences including aggression,
perception, learning, memory, motivation, emotion, psychopathology, development,
and the action of psychoactive drugs.
Note: If you have taken PSY 403 you cannot take this course.
Prerequisite: PSY 100 or COG 166.
Offered: Spring.
