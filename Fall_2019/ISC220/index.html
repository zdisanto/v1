Consideration of the basic principles and tools for analysis and retrieval of
information in various information systems (both textual and database systems).
Topics include analysis and storage of information, retrieval concepts and types
of retrieval systems.
Prerequisite: CSC 241 or ISC 250, may be taken concurrently; or instructor permission.
Offered: Fall
