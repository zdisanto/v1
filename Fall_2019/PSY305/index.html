This area of psychology is guided by the information processing approach in
its examination of how people think. Topics include attention and memory,
problem solving, decision making, and logical reasoning.
Note: If you have taken PSY 405 you cannot take this course.
Prerequisite: PSY 100 or COG 166
Offered: Fall, Spring
