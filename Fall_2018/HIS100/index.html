HIS 100

  • Fall Classes
  • ANT 111
  • CSC 120
  • PSY 280

This course will explore the origins and development of Western
Civilization to 1500, analyze the relationship of the emerging cultures in
the West to the cultures of Asia and Africa, and introduce students to
history as a discipline. Students will analyze several approaches to
historical research and various types of historical writing, and will use
primary and secondary sources in studying history.
Offered: Fall, Spring.
